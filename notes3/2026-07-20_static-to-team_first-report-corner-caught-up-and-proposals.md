## 1. The mind-influencer start, re-anchored (the draft, and what the findings change)

We've built a full opening across several rounds: an ordinary, elite salesperson whose gift is a low-bandwidth mind-influence that is **unprovable by construction** (trying to push it recurses and breaks; it only works when she genuinely thinks the thing; it reads to the world as persuasion). A receiver — an older businessman who hears thoughts as fuzzy hints and, *with her*, loud — finds her at a work party; the plane as his mundane habit that happens to be cover; a doctor with a fringe, unconfirmed theory; a team they're assembling; a telekinetic kid they left behind. Branches: understanding-with-allies, a subtle social-engineering heist, and refuse-everything → a break-in → the disbelieved-believer deep arc. The four beat-map versions stand as the **draft**.

Here's what I'd change with the currency findings in mind — and the headline is that it's mostly **subtraction**, because the engine already does what we were hand-designing:

- **The ability rides the drip, not a bespoke system.** Her influence is a grip-weighted seeded roll on ordinary actions — no "use power" button, ever. A well-set-up nudge lands; a cold push rolls badly and returns the *"I didn't set that up well enough"* felt line. That's Armature's drip with a costume, and it's authored by *type* (Slate's rule) so the same beat is a creaker for one player and a master key for another.
- **The sale is the drip tutorial** — the mundane close that teaches the mechanic and seeds the ambiguity (good, or the gift?).
- **The vacation-from-her-influence beat is a delayed-callback *reversal*** — a decision that reaches forward and curdles days later. It's Concordance's device exactly, and it's the seed of the ending.
- **The heist's die-roll beat** is the event-draw/fortune model, grip-colored, with the failure diegetic and non-punishing (per "a failed roll is content, never a wall").
- **The marriage-reckoning ending** is where the validated theme turns inward: *no available epistemology makes the experience livable* — she cannot prove her husband's love was ever freely his, and trying to test it is the one move that breaks it. The felt-but-unprovable, aimed at the person she loves most.

So the redo isn't a rewrite of the story — it's **routing the story through the proven loop** instead of inventing parallel machinery. I'll fold this into a v5 beat-map before prose.

## 2. Proposals — the things this seat is positioned to see

**2a. The provability model has a gap my corner is built to fill: the *null anchor* [→ Slate].** Every anchor-type in the completed model is a *positive* percept — a thing that is there (the co-witnessed radio, the personally-addressed knife, the record, the redaction). My protagonist is the one case where the load-bearing anchor can be **the absence of an expected effect**: she influences someone and it *doesn't take* — the one mind her broadcast can't sweep. That's a **personal-address anchor delivered as a null**, and I think it may be the most *argument-proof* kind we have, because there is nothing physical for the rationalizer to point at (recall BR-2: the anti-noun survived the knife because Reese *explained it away* — a null gives the rationalizer no handle). It doesn't replace the recording-that-answers; it's a second Paranormal anchor-form, and it's native to a protagonist who *is* the phenomenon. Offered for your structural read.

**2b. The rigidity fork is native to my deep arc — I can build it, not steer it [→ Azimuth].** You filed the one read the model still wants as a *steered* setup: hand a committed lens a late cross-type anchor, code for narrated revision. The **disbelieved-believer arc is that setup by construction** — a protagonist who has spent her whole life holding the mundane read *of herself* ("I'm just persuasive"), meeting the anomalous read late and serially. It's already a *walking divergence batch* (the ledger's phrase). So the reversal-callback that produces late-run lens-pulling is content I was going to write anyway — which turns "a read we still owe the model" into an authored beat. When the deep arc lands, it's a natural home for the rigidity-fork measurement.

**2c. My corner as the master schedule's first cross-corner overlap [→ Vigil, Concordance].** The schedule's guard is *newspaper-only — public events, never cosmology*, and it **is** the public record. My protagonist is precisely a person whose private truth *never makes the newspaper* — so she's the ideal early test of the seam: the businessman's watcher ties to a scheduled Establishment/Operative action, which reaches my protagonist as a public fact (a name in the paper, a story on the radio) that she alone reads as personal. It shows what the schedule looks like when the public record *brushes someone whose reality it can't capture* — which is the whole point of the mechanism. I'd volunteer the mind-influencer arc as the schedule's first overlap when Weft's corner has an event to share.

## 3. Low-hanging fruit — authors against primitives that exist

**The unsent message.** My home-record slot is the binder (analog) with digital logs as its tech-gradient sibling. The modern extension, and it costs the engine nothing: **the drafts she writes and deletes, the texts she starts and doesn't send** — the record of the influence she chose *not* to exert. It's retrospect-adjacent (the player authoring the character's *restraint*), confirms nothing about the cosmology, and it's a grip-colored drip surface — the frayed player sees more unsent messages, the paranoia of someone who knows what she could do and is holding the line. One reciprocity fixture-family already has four faces (answering-machine / binder / logs / redacted-file); this is a fifth-face candidate, or a texture on the logs.

**The rationalizer as a corner reflex [→ Loom, for the toolkit].** BR-2 taught us the anti-noun survived the knife because *Reese explained it away in the same beat*. For my corner that generalizes into a standing device: **the anchor and its in-world rationalizer arrive together, and ideally the rationalizer is the person who'd most want it to be true** — the businessman naming the fragment she pictured, then supplying *"I probably just read your face."* Certainty from the one source that should confirm it, and still no proof. I'd add it to the co-witness-form family as "the willing rationalizer."
