## §1 — The dossier as substrate, locked
- **Same world (Dean).** The "Order vs Establishment" dossier is the author-truth **substrate** the game's compass draws from — not a separate project, and (per the metaphysics) literally the game's roots cosmology worked out in full.

- **The design thesis (the *why*).** Grounding every story in one deepest author-truth layer makes each story a **facet of one overarching narrative** — so a player plays once, is enticed to replay, and comes to realize a single playthrough was never the intended way in. **Recognition across runs is the reward.** The substrate is the invisible unifier that pays off in replay. ## §2 — The Establishment, characterized · [BURIED]
- **A parallel, not a breakaway (Dean).** The institutional/technological side is **embedded in and running alongside the normal world** — *not* a hidden hyper-advanced civilization. Its tech edge is modest and defensively motivated: aggressively technological mainly to **counter the abilities** of the people it hunts, which may buy it a slight advantage. Keeps the world mundane-monstrous and plausible.

This is the dossier's shell + Containment-Management/Security-Directorate compartmentalization, **distributed across the map.**
- **The "Core Irony" (thematic spine, from the dossier).** It sees itself as protecting humanity from monsters and does not realize it **shapes the conditions that create them** — the same shape as the cult's wake (the containment-and-collection apparatus produces the broken and the dangerous). ## §3 — The Duggan founding myth + its modern skin
- **Historical (circa 1890s) · [BURIED] substrate.** **Duggan** — the Establishment's founder, **distinct from Doug the mentor** (§6) — captured someone able to **identify resources within the earth remotely**, and **founded a town** on the proceeds. He then used that ability as the hidden engine behind **false contraptions**: machines he could give *seemingly legitimate demonstrations* with, because the knowledge (where the resources were) had been **supplied in advance** by the gifted captive. It was presented to **Dutch royalty first** — the key early buyers — which triggered **World's-Fair / elite chatter** and spread it worldwide. This is the origin of the **Duggan–Dutch Industries (DDI)** "materials pickaxe" lineage.
- **Modern skin · candidate arc (parked).** The player can be led to **front a fabricated technical breakthrough** — a mathematical pattern, a computer chip, a novel higher-dimensional computing method — pitched to wealthy investors, where the "breakthrough" is really a **cover for someone's ability.** A **BMU story** (a gift monetized behind fabricated legitimacy) in modern dress. *Historical → compass; modern arc → Slate's lot / Loom when a corner reaches. Not to build now.*

## §4 — The composition & navigation model — **CORRECTED**
*(This supersedes the earlier note, which had the breadth-depth relationship reversed.)*
- **More sectors = deeper, longer arcs — not shallower.** A **straight shot** (e.g., Explorer → center) is the short, narrow path to a deep ending. A **spiral** — curving left/right into adjacent sectors as the player is pulled by interest, swirling around the center — is the **longer, deeper** arc that accumulates more sectors and more lenses on the way down. This **coheres with existing canon**: Loom's web already holds that curves are the texture and straight shots go tonally flat, and Slate's R5 holds that arcing earns breadth and reaches depth from a different angle. The earlier tension-flag to Vigil is **withdrawn** — the model was never in tension; it had been inverted.
- **The mechanics (Dean).** Heading toward center in one sector, the player talks to someone out of interest → drifts a bit left (or right) into a different story; talking to someone the other way **hooks them back** → a spiral. The **reciprocity tethers (Imani / Walt / Lena)** are exactly this hand-off mechanism — the fixture that pulls you toward an adjacent corner.
- **Swirl-around-without-reaching.** The player can **circle the center** — a surfaced-but-still-deep understanding — without the deepest confirmation. On-theme with the anti-noun and the cult-block-as-mid-radius-terminus (you brush the deep truth without settling it).
- **Intercept / any-ending-from-any-start.** A spiraled path can **intercept a near-center ending's region** and reach that ending even if you didn't start in its corner — so a straight-shot Explorer's near-center ending is *also* reachable by someone who spiraled in from elsewhere. This realizes design-state §10 ("any ending from any start; near ones easier") in the geometry, and it supports **late catch-up** (jumping into other stories much later, close to the center).
- **"We will plant the narratives for this" (Dean).** The drift/hand-off seams are **authored deliberately.** *Route: Vigil (owns the map/spiral) + Loom (authors the hand-offs); connects to the dome-cluster composition and the reciprocity tethers.*

## §9 — Multi-sector spirals, the up/down axis, and replay (structural vision · no-rush)

- **Same shape, different sectors = a different story (shape-translation).** A narrative *shape* — the line traced from a starting sector across an adjacent one toward the opposite sector — produces a **different story when translated to a different starting sector**, because the sector dynamics differ (Explorer→…→Operative is a different story from Detective→…→Paranormal at the *same geometry*). This is the game's **reciprocity principle scaled up from individual fixtures/beats to whole arcs** — Loom's web already holds "the same beat sits at a different coordinate per origin"; this generalizes it to shapes. Fast-forwarded, the fleshed-out narrative map reads as **an intricate set of spirals with lines intersecting the spiral arms.**

- **The up/down (vertical) axis = Order ↔ Establishment · [Concordance's read → Vigil].** In the ratified diamond, the four playable corners are the equatorial **grip × sanction** plane; the **vertical (z, enable↔contain the gifted)** maps cleanly to **Order ↔ Establishment — Order = the enabling/protecting top; Establishment = the containing/suppressing bottom.** This makes the already-ratified z-axis examples legible: bible §10's **"protector network" (benevolent enabling) *is* the unnamed Order**, and its **"research center" (malignant containing) *is* an unnamed Establishment node.** *Clarification worth stating so no one trips on it:* this vertical is **enable/contain, distinct from sanction** — so an Establishment-aligned figure can sit in a *fringe* corner and an Order-aligned one in a *sanctioned* corner (the sanctioned men-in-black and the fringe gifted-protector are both valid). The benevolent↔malignant charge stays orthogonal — which is why the **cult** (malignant *enabling*) sits near the Order's *height* but is its moral inverse: structurally an enabler, never the Order. *Route: Vigil (map-owner) for the formal blessing — this is load-bearing map structure. Also grounds the flagship map's "±z sibling starts" as the Order/Establishment lean of a start.*

- **Ability-gain arcs seeded per sector.** Each sector offers a start where the character **gains abilities** — the same "gain the gift" shape yielding a unique story per corner (shape-translation again). Consistent with design-state §5 (ability is an acquirable trait reachable from *any* corner, not owned by the Paranormal one). *Route: future per-corner authoring.*

- **Replay as recognition → divergence (a concrete loop).** The intended multi-run experience: **run 1** — Explorer to the cult, learn it; **run 2** — same start, *"what if I'd chosen differently"* (variation within the arc); **run 3** — *"I've already been there,"* so the player checks contacts/the map for a thread they skipped, talks to someone they didn't, and **drifts left or right into a different arc** instead of straight in. This rides **machinery already in hand** — recurrence as the only signal, the **reciprocity tethers** as the "someone you didn't talk to last time" hand-off, and the phone/contacts surface — so it needs **no new engine**; echoes can optionally reinforce it later. It is the design thesis (§1) made into a play loop.
