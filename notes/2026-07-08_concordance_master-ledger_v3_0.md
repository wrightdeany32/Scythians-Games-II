## §1 — THE FOUR INVARIANTS (one negative space)
A hand reaching to add a `truth` enum, an endings codex, a `player.disposition` field, or a "you-are-them" card hits one of these. All four in the engine header (Armature/Plumb); Concordance logs the family.

1. **No-truth-state** — no stored *meaning*. Endings select off flags + coordinates, never a stored answer.
2. **No-catalog** — no shown *structure*. Surfaces show what you know, never what remains: no deck names, no completion meters, no unlock toasts, no endings collection; the map shows places known, never content availability. *(Enforced as an import discipline — surfaces import only `dayMenu`, `SceneScreen`, `log`, qualitative-status accessors.)*
3. **No-stored-disposition** — no stored *position*. The engine stores *events* (resolved-card coordinates) and derives the diamond position; it never persists a disposition coordinate. **Experiential corollary — the narrow door:** position is observational, touched only by soft draw-bias; a dedicated **ending-selector** is the *one* hard read of derived position; no ordinary card ever gates on it (that would be `player.disposition` by the back door *and* would corrupt Azimuth's measurements). Grip and tier are the only mechanical coordinates, and they're legitimate stats.
4. **No-meta-reveal** — no confirmed *meta*. The meta-story (§2.5) is all seed, no payoff card, ever. No card confirms the player is the entity/the roots. Seeded, implied, never stated — at the deepest scale of all.

**The conviction-voice principle** (percept-never-cause at its most delicate — governs §1's spirit in prose): when a line approaches what the entity *is, did, wants, or knows*, render the **player's felt conviction** ("you cannot shake the certainty that something reached") — never **narrator-fact about the entity** ("it reached, it knows"). The test: whose statement is it — the player's percept (unfalsifiable, allowed) or the entity's cause (confirming, forbidden). This turns the run's terminal from the *first answer* into the *last fork*: divergence survives the final line.

## §2 — THE COMPASS (sealed substrate)
*Governing law: the **anti-noun (R3)** — all of this arrives as vertigo, experience, and machinery, never a settling noun; even "the roots" is the deepest lens's name for a thing that recedes under every lens.*

**§2.1 — The deepest keystone: the roots ARE the player** [absolute center · no-meta-reveal · revealed almost never]. The in-world cosmology — the gifted's **roots/tendrils** reaching into a higher-dimensional geometry of possible futures, which they **navigate** by aligning their trajectory toward a future that plays out — is, at the deepest level, a **fictional metaphor for the player controlling the character.** The player sits in a dimension the character can't perceive and reaches in through choices; the old leader's foresight is a *refraction of what the player literally does to the game.* The cult's power source is, at bottom, the player — nothing in the fiction advances, or exists even as fiction, without the player choosing. A deepest towards-center ending may surface the "roots" as the **physicist/biologist lens's** reading, but it is *never* stated as "you are the roots." **Purpose:** to entice the player to question their own existence — the parallel between how they shape the character and how something might shape *them*, pointing at the real question marks reality holds (Bell/EPR, black-hole information, the Planck scale). The payoff is the player looking up from the screen; write toward it never.

**§2.2 — The roots cosmology.** Control and foresight are the same power — **navigation**, not force. **Power is bandwidth** (max bandwidth and still not winning — the god-mode guardrail). **Minimization is survival** (the *frugality* that makes the deterrent a knife, not a bullet). **Darkness is decoherence** (sensory input perturbs the alignment — the trance needs the dark; a headlamp is a mortal threat; *this is why the wrongness concentrates at White's Hall*). **The floor beneath the floor** (revealed almost never): psychological *framing* suppresses ability at the species level — possession, insight, the god-king, peak performance are one substrate under different frames; nobody in-world understands this, not the cult. **Radius scoping:** the cult-block is a **mid-radius terminus**, not the floor; most players stop there, so it must read as "the answer" to everyone who does.

**§2.3 — The world: two forces, labels dropped (the dossier substrate).** The **source-fiction dossier** ("Order vs Establishment") is the fully-worked substrate; its metaphysics *is* §2.2 (a probabilistic "waveform" + a non-local "appendage/roots into probability space"). **The names never surface** (design-state §14); the player meets **meshed, linked groups** and pieces them together. **The institutional immune system** (dossier *Establishment*) — an unnamed apparatus across agencies/corporations/contractors that identifies, contains, exploits, and neutralizes the gifted, *seeing itself as protecting humanity from monsters while shaping the conditions that create them.* **A parallel, not a breakaway**; embedded, its tech edge modest and counter-ability; **compartmentalized** across every sector (actors report up chains they can't see); its **shell ecosystem** (25+ named fronts — Pantheon Holdings, NeuroSync Labs, DDI, …) supplies the deniable contractors and the never-generic men-in-black layer, distributed and extensible. **The dispersed gifted networks** (dossier *Order*) — protection cells, old families, more connected (communicators) but geographically isolated. **Duggan** (institutional founder, ~1890s; *distinct from Doug the mentor*) built a **snake-oil founding myth** — a captured remote-resource-locator finding minerals while a fake contraption took the credit, sold first to Dutch royalty (→ the DDI "materials pickaxe" line); modern skin (parked arc): the player fronts a fabricated breakthrough covering a gift. **The cult** is a **rogue gifted lineage** — Order-side but a self-hidden pariah the wider community disdains; the men in black already **circle its direction without understanding it**; it is one of ~5 sector-contained dead-end mini-stories, the Explorer's the flagship.

**§2.4 — The sealed cast & mechanisms.** **Two leaders:** an **old** one — more powerful, of an **inbred bloodline**, trapped by his own power, **trance-bound in the cave adjacent to White's Hall** (he *is* the presence; the shard turns his attention; the knife is him), who **scarred Doug (~30y)**, **took Ellen→Tricia**, and groomed a **successor** (less powerful, therefore mobile/functional, **half serving the old man and half his own ambition** — which sharpens the frugality tell). **Tricia = Ellen** — taken because her **roots anchor toward leadership** (scouted/fostered, not weapon-grade); the empty grave holds a body never there; one fixture, two sightlines. **The knife (finalized):** lost years pre-game (**no seed, ever**); retrieved via foresight, placed mundane/deniable/exposure-silent; **keys to *his* state**; a **deterrent that already failed** (found on a *return*); **provoked, not looted**; two variants (deep / car-seat); the run's **closer**. **The wake (decline curve):** Ellen taken (~50y, whole) → **Dale** spared at the same incident (~50y, the earliest survivor) → Doug reached-for (~30y) → the player (today) — diminishing reach, the frugality tell given a timeline. **Life-support & caretakers:** the trance *is* life-support; the dark keeps it; maintenance-as-horror (swept threshold, caches, a cot, no light). Approach gradient: traces → the (unreadable) **warner** → the face in the dark. **Doug** met the **old** leader; direct canary; progressive incapacitation eases the timeline. **The research nodes split by lens:** Nora's center (the powers · spiritual · past) and a **surgeon's group** (the brain mechanism · physics · live), never connecting. **The mind-controlled choice** (offered vs made-for-you, stolen-agency budget ≤1). **Foresight vs agency** (each outcome-arc needs a default stance; within-run only).

**§2.5 — The meta-layer.** One entity through **different people's eyes** (vessels = different characters, one per run). **The entity is the player.** Never stated — seeded via the **▓▓** (the seam it can't read itself), **in-world lore** ("entities that appear human, unknown motive" = the player), **faction-scars** (a later vessel in a world an earlier one shaped), and the **fate-dial** (the recency window lengthening with depth = momentum through a person). Cross-run memory = optional artifacts. **No-meta-reveal** is the wall; Loom's neutral non-confirming echo is the pattern.

**§2.6 — The null-pole honesty** [the anti-noun at its floor]. Per the sealed cosmology **Dale is right — it's people** — and his grounded-human read is the *closest to true* of any frame. Yet it rides the **skeptic/null** lens (§5): the reading that adds no tilt, cannot form a filter bubble, and can never win. So the design says three things at once and states none: the clearest-eyed reading is the one the town **dismissed**; it's the reading that keeps you **unbubbled**; and it is the reading the game will **never confirm** — the man who aims truest dies not knowing he was right. The math (the null pole) and the meaning (the honest man can't be proven right) are the same object.

## §4 — CONVENTIONS & CRAFT RULES
- **Horror grammar** [LOCKED] — presence where there should be absence, absence where there should be presence, a skeptic explaining each away. Never a monster. (Knife ↔ empty grave.)
- **Percept, never cause / specify the percept, never the cause** — enrich what's *perceived* so different priors each claim it; never let narration adjudicate what *made* it. Answers **correct-answer gravity** (withholding a stimulus collapses careful readers to the mundane).
- **Conviction-voice, not narrator-fact** (§1) — the cosmology-adjacent rule; the terminal and the pressure beat's stage-3 opener are the sweep targets.
- **The null pole** — `skeptic` is the **zero vector**, not a fourth vertex (§5); dismissal *removes* tilt (buys the unbiased world) and can't bubble. Hold the vocabulary at **four** (one null pole + three positive directions); a fifth is a fourth positive vertex, opened only when a corner opens it.
- **Unfalsifiable feedback** — noisy, felt, unprovable (Doug's canary); keep clean feedback (rolls, spends) legible.
- **The frugality tell** — the anomaly is frugal; **extravagant = off-model = a tell the author is someone else** (the successor; institutional theater). Applied to *content itself*: the `took_shard` variant corrupts the breather deck by **exactly one card**.
- **Seed discipline** — warm what pays off by recognition (Tricia's name), cold what pays off by eruption (the knife). **Presented-but-unchosen options still seed** — every label is exposition (which is *why* labels stay neutral-valence — a valenced label editorializes even to players who refuse it, and would move the centroid by tilting rather than expressing).
- **Neutral labels protect the centroid** — the choice states the action; unease lives in the narration. **Two channels:** what you *do* (neutral action) and how you *feel* (valenced introspection) both steer disposition; neither manipulates.
- **Grip-banded both ways** — grounded reveals the exit, frayed the way in; fraying opens as much as it closes. Descent register: `bandText{grounded, worn(4–6, the default), frayed}`.
- **The anti-noun of agency** — you cannot rescue the marked (Doug); the game never lets the player believe they could have. And the **never-returned ending** — the anti-noun's *quietest* form: the sensible life is real and survivable, **never a punishment for playing safe**.
- **The absence is the whole horror** — restraint as a *content rule*: no future pass elaborates the empty vault's interior; the absence is quiet.
- **Density / the casual-monstrous** — the horror is how *casual* the room is about a thing that should stop it (the cake, the observation meeting); offered to notice, never narrated at. It must *really be cake* — everyone is right, and the player feels what they feel anyway (the anti-noun on an emotion, not an event).
- **The patient door** — a met-door that lands, sits indefinitely, soft-re-nudges, never expires, gates *nothing* behind speed (the phone-as-hand).
- **Named beat-shapes** — the **anatomies** (A seed-and-return · B decompression · C tendril · D wake), **companion-reassurance-can-fail**, the **pressure beat** (bounded, traced, pre-frame), the **density flip**, the **quarry pattern**, **artifacts-of-the-deep** (each lens its artifact — shard/residue/relic/document — none confirming the substrate), **dual-use faction content** (believer's-eye and opponent's-eye from one material — the Z-axis exercised, stance-not-truth), **real-echoes-not-real-names** (a figure's ideas/ethos, never the name or an invented quote; the **echo-character demonstrates, never preaches** — his blindness authored as lovingly as his brilliance).
- **The authoring frame** — the tightening **spiral** (curves are the texture; straight shots go flat); dead-ends first-class; "find the spiral in the fog." **One fixture, many faces / reciprocity**; **shape-translation** (same arc, another start, a different story); professions portable across corners.
- **Anti-noun discipline** — one core sample; the cathedral stays a sketch; route design accumulation back to content.

## §5 — GEOMETRY & ENGINE ARCHITECTURE (summary; supersedes the standalone geometry doc's mechanics)
**The diamond.** Four **corners** on the equatorial **grip × sanction** plane (Detective/Operative/Explorer/Paranormal — a *position*, not a profession). The **vertical** = **enable ↔ contain the gifted** (worship→…→eradicate) = the **Order↔Establishment axis**. **Benevolent ↔ malignant** rides orthogonal (player freedom + perspective-flip). **Radius = depth.**
**The register skeleton** [the largest structural entry since the diamond] — the four corners are **four doors into one world**; each archetype *leads* with the register that fits its life (Detective/institutional, Operative/clandestine, Paranormal/spiritual, Explorer/full spread) but holds **all** registers underneath. **Precision 1:** the lead register is content **density**, *never* an engine coupling (spaces stay orthogonal — nobody wires corner→lens). **Precision 2:** registers ⊇ lensFlavors; a corner's lead register goes live as a flavor only when that corner's content arrives (the roadmap is the airlock).
**Emergent position** [KEYSTONE] — the recency-weighted **centroid** of resolved-card coordinates, derived, **never stored**. Trajectory (straight-shot vs spiral) is the centroid's path.
**Two coordinate spaces, one primitive** — **diamond** (where you stand) and **lens** (how you read: `spiritual · physics · institutional · skeptic`), orthogonal; `draw = weight × proximity_diamond × proximity_lens × recency`. **X = grip** (the stat *and* the grounded↔attuned axis; Y/Z/lens emergent; only grip+tier are mechanical). *Grip's "skeptic" ≠ the lens's institutional-skeptical: grip = do you sense the uncanny; lens = how you explain it.* **The null pole:** a `skeptic` resolution appends the **zero vector** (others one-hot); the lens centroid decays toward origin under skeptic play (multipliers ~1.0). Lens **is** the frame layer where divergence lives.
**The draw pipeline:** `Mount → Filter (tier ∧ tags ∧ requires ∧ date) → Weight → Draw (seed-deterministic | nextQueuedEvent) → Resolve-noise-once (band-select, frozen, {trueBand,resolvedBand}→trace) → Apply + record.` **Cards** carry `diamondCoord {sanction, vertical}` + `lensFlavor` + `tier`; locations add `mapPos` (**map ≠ diamond**, two systems). **Six atoms + fixtures** (entities are fixture-decks, not a 7th type; **subtypes** = multi-deck/group membership). **Creation loop = turn-zero** (the questionnaire/creation cards seed both centroids).
