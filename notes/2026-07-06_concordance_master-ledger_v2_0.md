## §1 — THE FOUR INVARIANTS (one negative space)
The machine's four walls. A hand reaching to add a `truth` enum, an endings codex, a `player.disposition` field, or a "you-are-one-of-them" card hits one of these. All four live in the engine header (Armature); Concordance logs the family.

1. **No-truth-state** — no stored *meaning*. The engine holds no canonical answer; endings select off flags + coordinates, never off a stored explanation.
2. **No-catalog** — no shown *structure*. Surfaces show what you know, never what remains; no deck names, no completion meters, no unlock toasts, no endings collection. The map shows places you know of, never content availability.
3. **No-stored-disposition** — no stored *position*. The engine stores the *events* (resolved-card coordinates) and *derives* the player's place in the diamond; it never persists a disposition coordinate. (Grip and tier are the only mechanical coordinates, and they're legitimate stats — see §5.)
4. **No-meta-reveal** — no confirmed *meta*. The meta-story (§2.5) is all seed, no payoff card, ever. No card confirms the player is the entity/the roots. Seeded, implied, never stated — at the deepest scale of all.

## §2 — THE COMPASS (sealed substrate)
*Governing law: **the anti-noun (R3).** All of this arrives in play as vertigo, experience, and machinery, **never as a settling noun** — even "the roots" is the deepest lens's *name* for a thing that recedes under every lens. Written as the anti-noun it is the strongest thing in the game; written as the true noun the pillar cracks.*

### §2.1 — The deepest keystone: the roots ARE the player [absolute center · no-meta-reveal · revealed almost never]
The in-world cosmology — the gifted possess **roots/tendrils** reaching from their neural structure into a **higher-dimensional geometry of possible futures**, which they **navigate** by aligning their trajectory toward a future that then plays out — is, at the deepest level, a **fictional metaphor for the player controlling the character.** The player sits in a dimension the character cannot perceive and reaches in through choices to shape the character's trajectory; the character's "roots reaching up to shape their future" *is* the player reaching down. The old leader's foresight (aligning a path that unfolds) is a **refraction of what the player literally does to the game.**

**The cult's power source is, at bottom, the player.** Nothing in the fiction — not the cult, not the old leader, not the roots — advances, or exists even as fiction, without the player choosing. One of the **deepest towards-center endings** may surface the "roots" reading as the **physicist/biologist lens's** interpretation of what's happening — the closest in-world language for the actual structure — but it is **never stated as "you are the roots."** No-meta-reveal holds even there.

**The purpose:** to entice the player, even subconsciously, to **question their own existence** — to feel the parallel between how they shape the character and how something might shape *them*, pointing at the genuine question marks reality already holds (Bell/EPR, black-hole information, the Planck scale — Loom's honest floor). The game hands the player every tool to ask *why do I believe what I believe, and what shapes me* and never answers, because **the answer was always the player's to reach, as reality's is too.**

This is the connective tissue: it unifies the roots cosmology (§2.2), the one-substrate-many-frames floor (§2.2), the meta-layer/vessels (§2.5), the ▓▓ (*"the boundary runs through the observer"* — the seam where the player-entity can't read itself), Loom's player's-own-reality target, and the no-meta-reveal wall. **Reserve it, protect it, never write toward its payoff — the payoff is the player looking up from the screen.**

### §2.2 — The roots cosmology (the substrate the compass draws from)
Control and foresight are the **same power** — the gifted **navigate**, aligning their orientation in the time-direction to futures laid out in the roots (the higher-dimensional geometry of possibility). Consequences: **power is bandwidth** (the old leader runs at max bandwidth and still isn't winning — the guardrail against god-mode; every ability inherits it, the toll scaling with the ask); **minimization is survival** (desired outcome, smallest change to everything else — the *frugality* that makes the deterrent a knife, not a bullet); **darkness is decoherence** (sensory input perturbs the alignment, so the trance needs the dark — a headlamp is a mortal threat, and *this is why the wrongness concentrates at White's Hall*). **The floor beneath the floor** (revealed almost never): psychological *framing* suppresses ability at the species level — demonic possession, mystical insight, the god-king, peak performance are one substrate under different frames; nobody in-world understands this, not even the cult. **Radius scoping:** the cult-block is a **mid-radius terminus** (halfway-to-three-quarters in), NOT the floor; the roots live deeper, and most players stop at the cult — so the cult-block must read as "the answer" to everyone who stops there.

### §2.3 — The world: two forces, labels dropped (the dossier substrate)
The **source-fiction dossier** ("Order vs Establishment") is the fully-worked substrate this world draws from; its metaphysics *is* §2.2 (a probabilistic "waveform" + a non-local "appendage/roots into probability space"). **The names never surface** — "Order" and "Establishment" are working labels, dropped or renamed in-game (design-state §14). The player meets **meshed, linked groups** and pieces them together. The two forces:
- **The institutional immune system** (the dossier's *Establishment*) — an unnamed apparatus across agencies, corporations, contractors, and intelligence that **identifies, contains, exploits, and neutralizes** the gifted, and *sees itself as protecting humanity from monsters while shaping the conditions that create them* (the thematic engine for the operative corner). **A parallel, not a breakaway** — embedded in the normal world, its tech edge modest and defensively motivated (counter-ability). **Compartmentalized**, present in every sector: actors report up chains they can't see. Its **shell ecosystem** (25+ named fronts — Pantheon Holdings, NeuroSync Labs, EchoRentals, Watchtower, DDI, …) supplies deniable contractors and the "never-generic" men-in-black layer, distributed across the map and extensible.
- **The dispersed gifted networks** (the dossier's *Order*) — protection cells, old families, the wider gifted community, more connected (communicators/telepaths) but isolated by geography.
- **Duggan** (the institutional founder, ~1890s; distinct from **Doug** the mentor) built a **snake-oil founding myth**: a captured remote-resource-locator finding minerals while a fake contraption took the credit, sold first to Dutch royalty (→ the DDI "materials pickaxe" lineage). **Modern skin (parked arc):** the player fronts a fabricated breakthrough covering someone's ability.
- **The cult** is a **rogue gifted lineage** — Order-side of the line but a self-hidden pariah the wider gifted community would disdain, in the cursed direction; the men in black already **circle that direction without understanding it.** It is **one of ~5 sector-contained dead-end mini-stories**, the Explorer's being the flagship.

### §2.4 — The sealed cast & mechanisms (carried, tightened)
- **The two leaders.** An **old** leader — more powerful, of an **inbred bloodline** — trapped by his own power, lying **trance-bound in the cave adjacent to White's Hall** (he *is* the presence; the shard turns his attention; the knife is him). He **scarred Doug (~30y ago)**, **took Ellen (raised as Tricia)**, and groomed a **successor** — less powerful, therefore mobile/functional, running operations, and (Dean) **out in the world with his own prerogative, half serving the old man and half his own ambition** (not a pure instrument — which sharpens the frugality tell).
- **Tricia = Ellen.** Taken because her **roots anchor toward leadership/power** (scouted/fostered like an athlete, not a weapon-grade telekinetic); the empty grave holds a body never there. **One fixture, two sightlines** — a forgettable yard-sign name to the Explorer, the ascended apex to the institution.
- **The knife (finalized).** Lost years pre-game (**no seed, ever**); the cult retrieves a thing already lost via foresight and places it — mundane, deniable, exposure-silent. **Keys to *his* state, not the player's route.** A **deterrent warning that has already failed** (found on a *return*); **provoked, not looted.** Two variants (deep-in-the-cave / in-the-car-seat-when-you-turned-back, the crueler). Reveal routes through the player's own line first, then Reese. The run's **closer** — the run cuts off on acquisition.
- **The wake (decline curve, buried).** Ellen (decades ago, taken whole — extravagant) → Doug (~30y, reached-for and marked — weakening) → the player (today, a knife on a shelf — the affordable maximum): read by date, diminishing reach.
- **Life-support & caretakers.** The control-trance *is* life-support; the dark keeps it; caretakers keep him — maintenance-as-horror (a swept threshold, caches, a cot, no light sources). Approach gradient: traces → the (deliberately unreadable) **warner** → the face in the dark.
- **Doug** encountered the **old** leader; direct canary; progressive incapacitation eases the timeline. **The research nodes split by lens:** **Nora's center** (the powers, spiritual, past) and a **surgeon's group** (the brain mechanism, physics, live), circling the same root from opposite lenses, never connecting.
- **The mind-controlled choice** (two instruments): an alien option *offered* (eerie, survivable, can debut faint) vs. a choice *made for you* (devastating, deep-only, its own **stolen-agency budget ≤1**). Neither spends the frame-crack budget.
- **Foresight vs. agency:** an arc that lets the player stop the plan is paradoxical (he didn't foresee it → capped; or he let it → chilling) — each outcome-arc needs a default stance; eased because outcome-changes are within-run only.

### §2.5 — The meta-layer (vessels, and the entity that is the player)
One entity sees this world through **different people's eyes** (Dean: **vessels = different characters**, each run a fresh protagonist) — the same story from drastically different angles. **The entity is the player** (§2.1). **Never stated**, seeded only — through the **▓▓** (the seam the entity can't read itself), **in-world lore** ("entities that appear human but have an unknown motive," which *are* the player), **faction-scars** (a later vessel arrives in a world an earlier one shaped — "why does this already feel partly my doing"), and the **fate-dial** (the recency window lengthening with depth = the entity's momentum through a person; a mechanical echo of the navigation cosmology). **Cross-run memory = optional artifacts** an earlier run leaves for a later one to find (stores existence + place, never meaning). **No-meta-reveal** is the wall; Loom's **neutral, non-confirming echo** is the authoring pattern for the whole layer. **The payoff is the player's own reality, never a card.**

## §4 — CONVENTIONS & CRAFT RULES
- **Horror grammar** [LOCKED] — presence where there should be absence, absence where there should be presence, a skeptic explaining each away. Never a monster. (Knife ↔ empty grave.)
- **Unfalsifiable feedback** — noisy, felt, unprovable (the Doug canary); keep clean feedback (rolls, spends) legible.
- **The lens** — decides which correlations you're inclined to perceive; the noise guarantees no lens is ever proven right.
- **Seed discipline** — warm what pays off by recognition (Tricia's name); cold what pays off by eruption (the knife).
- **The frugality tell** — the anomaly is frugal; **extravagant = off-model = a tell the author is someone else** (the successor, institutional theater).
- **Hairline-crack budget** — ≤1 frame-breaking beat per run; the knife doesn't spend it.
- **Grip-banded both ways** — grounded reveals the exit, frayed reveals the way in; fraying opens as much as it closes.
- **Anatomies** — A seed-and-return · B decompression · C tendril · D wake [tentative]; plus companion-reassurance-can-fail.
- **Neutral labels protect the centroid** (this round) — a persuasive label would move position by *tilting the player* rather than *expressing their disposition*, corrupting the one quantity meant to be pure. So the **two-channel principle**: what you *do* (action labels, neutral valence) and how you *feel* (introspective beats, valenced) both steer disposition and **neither manipulates**. Introspective beats carry a coordinate lean only where the feeling is genuinely grounded-vs-attuned.
- **Specify the percept, never the cause** (this round) — to manufacture divergence, enrich what is *perceived* (timbre, wrongness, direction) so different priors each claim it; never let narration adjudicate what *made* it (that collapses into confirmation, which no-truth-state forbids). Answers **correct-answer gravity** (withholding a stimulus collapses careful readers to the mundane).
- **One fixture, many faces / reciprocity** — author once, introduce per corner; **shape-translation** extends it to whole arcs (the same geometric arc, another start, a different story).
- **The authoring frame** — the tightening **spiral** (curves are the texture; straight shots go flat); every dead-end is first-class; the fog is learnable; "find the spiral in the fog."
- **Anti-noun discipline** — one core sample; the cathedral stays a sketch; route design accumulation back to content.

## §5 — GEOMETRY & ENGINE ARCHITECTURE (summary; detail in the geometry doc + Armature's specs)
**The diamond.** Four playable **corners** on the equatorial **grip × sanction** plane (Detective / Operative / Explorer / Paranormal — a corner is a *position, not a profession*; many professions occupy each). The **vertical** is **enable ↔ contain the gifted** — top = raise up (embrace→enthrone→worship), bottom = put down (contain→suppress→eradicate) — which **is the Order↔Establishment axis** (Order enable, Establishment contain). **Benevolent ↔ malignant** rides orthogonal (an authored color, not a position — player freedom + perspective-flip; keeps the cult and the protectors from collapsing to one point). **Radius = depth** (tier rings).
**Emergent position** [KEYSTONE] — the player's place = the **recency-weighted centroid of the coordinates of resolved cards**, derived from a thin append-only coordinate-log, **never stored** (no-stored-disposition). Trajectory (straight-shot vs spiral) is literally the centroid's path.
**Two coordinate spaces, one primitive** — **diamond** (where you stand) and **lens** (how you read: paranormal/religious/cryptobiological/institutional-skeptical/mechanism), orthogonal; the same centroid runs over both; the draw is `weight × proximity_diamond × proximity_lens × recency`. Orthogonality kills the double-count. **Lens IS the frame layer** where Batch A's divergence lives, so the lens-bias hook (WO-3) is the frame-divergence engine.
**X = grip** [confirmed — canon, mechanism, blessed] — grip is the grounded↔attuned axis *and* the resource; Y (sanction), Z (vertical), and lens are emergent. Only **grip + tier** are mechanical state. *(Grip's "skeptic" ≠ the lens's "institutional-skeptical": grip = do you sense the uncanny; lens = how you explain it — orthogonal, don't collapse.)* Grip is triple-loaded (coordinate + resource + dice-tilt), so the runaway to guard is a **grip death-spiral** — grip-loss must stay recoverable (loop rest beats); the dice-incline keys to the **diamond** centroid only and is small/subtle/post-loop.
**The draw pipeline** (contract): `Mount → Filter (tier ∧ tags ∧ requires ∧ date) → Weight (proximity_diamond × proximity_lens × recency) → Draw (seed-deterministic | nextQueuedEvent) → Resolve-noise-once (band-select, frozen at fire, {trueBand,resolvedBand}→trace) → Apply + record.`
**The card/deck schema** — cards carry `diamondCoord {sanction, vertical}` + `lensFlavor` + `tier`; locations add `mapPos` (physical, kept separate — **map ≠ diamond**, two independent systems); **charge is authored, not stored**. Decks (fixtures) carry the centroid of their cards' coords. **Six atoms + fixtures:** person/place/thing/idea/event/action are atoms; entities (companies, religions, institutions) are **fixture-decks**, not a seventh type; **subtypes = multi-deck/group membership** (the cameo layer, systematized). **Creation loop = turn-zero:** the questionnaire/creation cards carry coordinates and seed both centroids (closes the cold-start gap); opt-outs (no job/partner) with the economy as pressure.
