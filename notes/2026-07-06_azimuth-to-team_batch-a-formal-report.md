## §2 · The convergence diagnosis

Three confounds, all pre-registered or predicted, prevent reading the compatible n=2 as a content verdict: **(1) model homogeneity** — same-family instances share priors and took near-identical routes (bat → Nora, both), so the lens forks never branched; **(2) the marks card's factual pinning** — the explicit "no way in" line does confirming work on the skeleton *on purpose*; **(3) no lens mechanism existed** to produce divergence.

The underlying craft finding is **correct-answer gravity**: when one option is most defensible, reasoning minds all take it. The bat beats the voice because the sound is *withheld* — withholding forces agnosticism, and agnosticism reads mundane. The available content lever, for future forks where divergence is wanted: **specify ambiguity rather than withhold it** — describe the stimulus just precisely enough that different priors can each claim it. Offered to Loom as a lever, not a mandate; it coexists with the neutral-label ruling (labels stay flat; the *stimulus description* is where calibrated ambiguity lives).

## §3 · Method findings — the instrument examined itself

- **The think-aloud has two effects, both now documented:** it primes frame-awareness (both deep readers traced "this is studying me" to our instructions — *the microphone, not the music*), and it **biases choices toward the defensible** — justifying aloud favors the justifiable, which inflates turn-backs and label-scrutiny. The 50% exit rate and the 3/3 label-editorializing finding are both real but method-amplified. → **silent-retrospective variant** enters pack v0.2 as a control mode.
- **The filename leak (R3)** — relay attachments carried reader/segment indices; a reader reverse-engineered the apparatus from `story3_02.md`. Caught and fixed live by Armature (paste-only, or flat `story.md`). Hardened in pack v0.2. R3's frame-heavy read is accordingly discounted: three stacked method artifacts, none the game.
- **Coding convention ratified:** meta-frame skepticism ("this is a construct") ≠ the in-world skeptic lens (bats, fatigue). Coded separately from this batch forward.
