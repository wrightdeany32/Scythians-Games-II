## ¬ß3 ‚Äî What the engine must grow (all additive, all inside the walls)

- **A ¬∑ Branch-level fields** ‚Äî ¬ß1. *(The gate.)*

Needed for the conditional-insert pattern: the return queues `[illegible, fork]` and the illegible card's `grip ‚â§ 3` condition lets `nextQueuedEvent`'s existing skip do the routing; the research actions queue `[deeper, first]` with complementary `theory` conditions the same way.
- **C ¬∑ Numeric flag counters** ‚Äî an `addFlags?: Record<string, number>` outcome verb (numeric add, creating at 0) and a `{kind:"counter", flag, op, value}` condition. The research engine's `theory_*` ladders ("advances `theory_spiritual`", "requires `theory_physics >= 2`") are counters, and today flags only set-absolute and compare-equal.
- **D ¬∑ Conditional card text** ‚Äî two small presentation-time fields, conditions evaluated once at fire (frozen, deterministic, no RNG): `bodyVariants?: {when, text}[]` (first match replaces the body ‚Äî the return's `took_shard` / not-shard erased-scar and knife beats) and `bodyExtras?: {when, text}[]` (every match appends ‚Äî the ending's thread-echoes, the pressure stages' shard strands, the `dale_bond` easing). - **E ¬∑ Staged exposure** ‚Äî `tuning.exposure.stages?: {at, eventId}[]`: each fires once, in order, queue-deduped, checked at the day boundary beside the existing single-threshold path (which stays for compat). Stage 3's plateau is free ‚Äî a fired stage never refires.

## ¬ß4 ‚Äî ‚õî The one schema conflict, flagged loudly: "leans *attuned*" has nowhere to go

Every introspective option in the pack carries `diamondCoord` leans named **grounded ‚Üî attuned** ‚Äî the return's windshield beats, the pressure reactions, the ending's two closing postures, Dale's wariness. But grounded‚Üîattuned is the **X axis**, and X is **grip** by ratified ruling ‚Äî which is exactly why `DiamondCoord` is `{sanction, vertical}` (Y, Z) *only*, so grip can never reach the draw. These options also say, correctly and deliberately, **"narration only, no stat"** ‚Äî so they can't be grip deltas either (that would be mechanical, farmable, and against their own design text). As written, the attuned/grounded leans have **no field to land in**: the enable-pole leans (the dinner, the meeting) wire cleanly to Z, but the X-leans are unwireable without a decision. The missing ledger v3.0 may already answer this ‚Äî its manifest entry claims the coord conventions ‚Äî but the folder copy doesn't exist to check. Options, my lean last:

1. **Drop the coordinate on X-lean introspectives** (prose only). Cheapest; loses the "final posture" signal the ending explicitly wants to feed "the final centroid."
2. **Make them grip deltas.** Violates their own "no stat" design, makes introspection farmable, couples X back toward the machinery the walls were built to keep it out of.
3. **An `attune?: number` component on the coordinate log entry** ‚Äî derived by the same centroid primitive into an emergent *attunement posture* that **nothing in the draw or dice ever reads** (grip remains the only mechanical X, per the ruling and clarification 1). Additive, backfill-safe, and it gives the authored leans a home without touching a single guardrail ‚Äî the Weight step can't read it because it's never in `DiamondCoord`, by type.
4. **My lean is (3)** ‚Äî but this is a cross-seat call (it grazes the X=grip ruling and the no-stored-disposition family), so: **Vigil to rule, with Slate/Armature/Azimuth's takes, before the coordinate wiring starts.** The prose wires regardless, exactly as the manifest says.
