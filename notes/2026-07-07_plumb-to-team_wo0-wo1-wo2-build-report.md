## ┬з2 тАФ Decisions taken under latitude (the review checklist)

1. **The recency ordinal is a global resolution clock.** `resolveCount` ticks on *every* card/action resolution; log entries stamp the clock. So neutral play pushes old coordinates into the past тАФ "window in cards" reads as *cards resolved*, not *coordinated cards resolved*. The kernel (`e^(тИТage/window)`) and window (`tuning.disposition.window`, default **12 тАФ a placeholder**) are one function and one knob; the fate-dial rides the knob later.
2. **Entry-action rolls are now continued and narrated.** The old Session silently *dropped* an entry action's roll (win/lose never applied). The SceneRunner folds opening narration from the log delta under one rule, continues the roll, and shows a refused (too-tired) entry honestly instead of presenting success flavor. No shipped content hits any of these paths тАФ `cave-b3` regenerates byte-identical тАФ but it is a deliberate contract change, on the record here.
3. **The terminal contract.** Terminals take effect **when control returns to the day** (surfaced on `dayMenu.status`, checked by `advanceDay`), not mid-card тАФ so an authored pull-back-from-the-brink inside one scene remains content's prerogative, and a run that comes back to the day lost is over before anything else happens. Driven in the harness (a mid-scene `taken` completes its scene, then ends the run at the menu).
4. **A zero-weighted pool draws nothing.** Previously `weightedPick`'s fallback returned the pool's tail card even at weight 0 тАФ anti-repeat with factor 0 inverted into forced repetition once the pool was exhausted. Now: honest silence until the memory rotates.
5. **The cross-run store contributes scars, not shape.** `newGame` starts from `db.factions` and applies the store's ratings only to factions content still knows тАФ content added between vessels exists at authored power; content removed doesn't resurrect. (The naive overlay threw mid-card on a clash with a post-harvest faction.)
6. **Doors sweep inside `endDay`**, beside the exposure consequence and the scheduled sweep тАФ everything that fires "when the day turns" at one depth, so every driver gets identical mornings. Both queue-pushes dedupe to one pending copy.
7. **`runAction` refuses while a morning beat is queued** ("scene pending") тАФ otherwise the pending card fires under the action's opening narration.
8. **Truthy flag semantics** on `mountFlag` and terminal flags, matching `{kind:"flag"}` without `equals` тАФ documented; keep thread/terminal flags boolean. If content wants condition-mounted decks later, `mount?: Condition` is an additive change.
9. **Dropped `tuning.dailyDraw`** (I had added it speculatively; nothing consumed it). The ambient-draw shape belongs to the loop-content decision when Loom's scenes land тАФ a knob wired to nothing is a trap.
10. **Numbers that are placeholders, awaiting the post-loop budget:** disposition window 12; diamondProximity strength 0.5 / range 1.5; antiRepeat factor 0.5 / memory 5. All ship off (the switches are the contract); none has been tuned against anything.
