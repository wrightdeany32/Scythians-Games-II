## §2 — THE COMPASS (sealed substrate)
*Governing law: the **anti-noun (R3)** — vertigo, experience, machinery, never a settling noun.*

**§2.1 — The deepest keystone: the roots ARE the player** [absolute center · no-meta-reveal]. The gifted's **roots** into a higher-dimensional geometry of futures, **navigated** by aligning one's trajectory, is a fictional metaphor for **the player controlling the character.** The cult's power source is, at bottom, the player. The payoff is the player looking up from the screen; write toward it never.

**§2.2 — The roots cosmology.** **Navigation**, not force. **Power is bandwidth** (the god-mode guardrail). **Minimization is survival** (the *frugality* tell). **Darkness is decoherence** (*why the wrongness concentrates at White's Hall*). **The floor beneath the floor**: framing suppresses ability at the species level — possession, insight, the god-king, peak performance are one substrate under different frames. **Radius scoping:** the cult-block is a **mid-radius terminus**; each corner has its own.

**§2.3 — The world: two forces, labels dropped (the dossier substrate).** The **institutional immune system** (dossier *Establishment*) — an unnamed apparatus that identifies/contains/exploits/neutralizes the gifted, *seeing itself as protecting humanity from monsters while shaping the conditions that create them* (the Operative corner's engine); compartmentalized; the shell ecosystem (Pantheon Holdings, NeuroSync Labs, DDI, …). **The dispersed gifted networks** (dossier *Order*). **Duggan** (~1890s, the snake-oil myth; *≠ Doug*). **The cult** — a rogue gifted lineage the men in black **circle without understanding**; one of ~5 sector-contained dead-ends, the Explorer's the flagship.

**§2.5 — The meta-layer, and the watcher's thread** [the deepest structure in the game].
