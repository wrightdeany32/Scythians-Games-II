## §1 — THE FOUR INVARIANTS (one negative space) + their guards
A hand reaching to add a `truth` enum, an endings codex, a `player.disposition` field, or a "you-are-them" card hits one of these. All four in the engine header (Plumb); Concordance logs the family.
1. **No-truth-state** — no stored *meaning*. Endings select off flags + coordinates, never a stored answer.
2. **No-catalog** — no shown *structure*. Surfaces show what you know, never what remains. Enforced by the **surface import allowlist** — now **compile-time**: the renderer receives a `Surface` type that *omits* the trajectory, exposure, grip-as-a-number, NPC ability/loyalty, and the queue; an **import-boundary linter** fails the build on any other engine import from a renderer. The forbidden numbers aren't un-read; they're **unreachable**.
3. **No-stored-disposition** — no stored *position*. Events stored (resolved-card coordinates), the diamond position derived, never persisted. **The narrow door:** a dedicated **ending-selector** is the *one* hard read; no ordinary card gates on it. **Structural** (see #2 — the renderer cannot reach the trajectory). Grip + tier are the only mechanical coordinates.
4. **No-meta-reveal** — no confirmed *meta*. The meta-story (§2.5) is all seed, no payoff card, ever. *(Realized, held: Denise's cross-run collision surfaces the entity meeting the man it destroyed as nameless, unplaceable feeling, gone before it can be examined.)*

**Guards beside the invariants:** **conviction-voice** (near the cosmology, render the player's *felt certainty*, never *narrator-fact about the entity*); **the deduction-verifier anti-model** (no verifier ever; theories live in the player, never marked correct; **know-vs-conclude is the boundary**; `theory_*` counters track *investment*, never *correctness*).

## §2 — THE COMPASS (sealed substrate)
*Governing law: the **anti-noun (R3)** — vertigo, experience, machinery, never a settling noun; even "the roots" recedes under every lens.*

**§2.1 — The deepest keystone: the roots ARE the player** [absolute center · no-meta-reveal]. The in-world cosmology — the gifted's **roots/tendrils** into a higher-dimensional geometry of possible futures, **navigated** by aligning one's trajectory — is a fictional metaphor for **the player controlling the character.** The old leader's foresight is a refraction of what the player literally does; the cult's power source is, at bottom, the player. A deepest ending may surface "the roots" as the physicist/biologist reading, never as "you are the roots." Purpose: to entice the player to question their own existence, pointing at reality's real question marks. The payoff is the player looking up from the screen; write toward it never.

**§2.2 — The roots cosmology.** Control and foresight are one power — **navigation**, not force. **Power is bandwidth** (the god-mode guardrail). **Minimization is survival** (the *frugality* — the deterrent is a knife, not a bullet). **Darkness is decoherence** (*this is why the wrongness concentrates at White's Hall*). **The floor beneath the floor** (revealed almost never): psychological *framing* suppresses ability at the species level. **Radius scoping:** the cult-block is a **mid-radius terminus**; most players stop there, so it must read as "the answer."

**§2.3 — The world: two forces, labels dropped (the dossier substrate).** The **source-fiction dossier** ("Order vs Establishment") is the fully-worked substrate; its metaphysics *is* §2.2. **The names never surface**; the player meets **meshed, linked groups**. **The institutional immune system** (dossier *Establishment*) — an unnamed apparatus across agencies/corporations/contractors that identifies, contains, exploits, neutralizes the gifted, *seeing itself as protecting humanity from monsters while shaping the conditions that create them.* A **parallel, not a breakaway**; **compartmentalized**; its **shell ecosystem** (Pantheon Holdings, NeuroSync Labs, DDI, …) supplies the deniable contractors and never-generic men-in-black layer. **The dispersed gifted networks** (dossier *Order*). **Duggan** (institutional founder, ~1890s; *distinct from Doug*) — the snake-oil founding myth → the DDI "materials pickaxe." **The cult** is a **rogue gifted lineage** — a self-hidden pariah the men in black **circle without understanding**; one of ~5 sector-contained dead-end mini-stories, the Explorer's the flagship.

**§2.4 — The sealed cast & mechanisms.** **Two leaders:** an **old** one — more powerful, of an **inbred bloodline** — **trance-bound in the cave adjacent to White's Hall** (he *is* the presence), who **scarred Doug (~30y)**, **took Ellen (→ Tricia)**, groomed a **successor** (mobile/functional, **half his own ambition**). **Tricia = Ellen** — taken because her **roots anchor toward leadership**. **The knife (finalized):** lost years pre-game (**no seed, ever**); retrieved via foresight, placed mundane/deniable; **keys to *his* state**; a **deterrent that already failed**; the run's **closer**. **The wake (decline curve):** Ellen taken + **Dale** spared at the same night (**~40y**) → **Doug** reached-for (**~30y**) → the player. **The shard = a recorded symbol/cipher:** the player **records** the cave sigil (a page — the copy no longer matches the wall on the return). **THE CIPHER PIN — ANCIENT-SOURCE [corrected from v3.2's center-made · sealed · Dean's landing per Loom's reconciliation]:** the cave sigil is **NOT** the center's notation — it is an **ancient mark of a *live* source**, put in the cave long ago **by the source itself**. The **research center merely *documents* it** (studied the source, recognized/recorded the sigil) — so **Nora's recognition runs the right way round**: she has **seen it before**, at the **church** and in the center's materials. The **cave is one older, more isolated instance** of a **recurring** mark (a more ancient version of what Nora saw at the church) — *the cave is not the source*; it's a place the source marked. **The source is LIVE** — which is **load-bearing**: it gives the copy-mismatch its *unstated reason* (the thing the mark marks isn't fixed, so a faithful copy can be wrong and you can't say since when — percept-never-cause at the deepest level; a static center-made carving wouldn't shift and the mismatch would lose its engine). *Reserved for Dean:* how far the **source/center/cult entanglement** goes — minimal pin = the center as **documenter** of an older phenomenon (set here); the bigger commitment (the center entangled with the cult's origin — the "creates the monsters it fears" thread, which hangs off the *source* either way).
