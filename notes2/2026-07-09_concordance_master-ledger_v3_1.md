## §1 — THE FOUR INVARIANTS (one negative space) + their guards
A hand reaching to add a `truth` enum, an endings codex, a `player.disposition` field, or a "you-are-them" card hits one of these. All four in the engine header (Plumb/Armature); Concordance logs the family.
1. **No-truth-state** — no stored *meaning*. Endings select off flags + coordinates, never a stored answer.
2. **No-catalog** — no shown *structure*. Surfaces show what you know, never what remains; no deck names, meters, unlock toasts, or endings collection; the map shows places known, never content availability. Enforced as a **surface import allowlist** (renderers import only `dayMenu`, `SceneScreen`, `log`, qualitative-status accessors).
3. **No-stored-disposition** — no stored *position*. The engine stores *events* (resolved-card coordinates) and derives the diamond position; never persists a disposition coordinate. **The narrow door:** a dedicated **ending-selector** is the *one* hard read of derived position; no ordinary card gates on it. Grip and tier are the only mechanical coordinates.
4. **No-meta-reveal** — no confirmed *meta*. The meta-story (§2.5) is all seed, no payoff card, ever.

**Guards logged beside the invariants** (the same negative space, named where a mechanic would breach it): **the conviction-voice principle** — near the cosmology, render the player's *felt certainty* ("you can't argue away the certainty that something reached"), never *narrator-fact about the entity* ("it reached, it knows"); the test is whose statement it is. **The deduction-verifier anti-model** — no verifier, ever; theories live in the player, never marked correct; the game records what you *know* (percepts/flags — the journal), never what you *conclude*; **know-vs-conclude is the boundary**, and `theory_*` counters track *investment*, never *correctness*.

## §2 — THE COMPASS (sealed substrate)
*Governing law: the **anti-noun (R3)** — vertigo, experience, machinery, never a settling noun; even "the roots" recedes under every lens.*

**§2.1 — The deepest keystone: the roots ARE the player** [absolute center · no-meta-reveal]. The in-world cosmology — the gifted's **roots/tendrils** reaching into a higher-dimensional geometry of possible futures, **navigated** by aligning one's trajectory toward a future that plays out — is a fictional metaphor for **the player controlling the character.** The old leader's foresight is a refraction of what the player literally does to the game; the cult's power source is, at bottom, the player (nothing advances, or exists even as fiction, without the player choosing). A deepest ending may surface "the roots" as the physicist/biologist reading, but never as "you are the roots." The purpose: to entice the player to question their own existence — pointing at reality's real question marks (Bell/EPR, black-hole information, the Planck scale). The payoff is the player looking up from the screen; write toward it never.

**§2.2 — The roots cosmology.** Control and foresight are one power — **navigation**, not force. **Power is bandwidth** (max bandwidth, still not winning — the god-mode guardrail). **Minimization is survival** (the *frugality* that makes the deterrent a knife, not a bullet). **Darkness is decoherence** (sensory input perturbs the alignment — the trance needs the dark; *this is why the wrongness concentrates at White's Hall*). **The floor beneath the floor** (revealed almost never): psychological *framing* suppresses ability at the species level — possession, insight, the god-king, peak performance are one substrate under different frames; nobody in-world understands this. **Radius scoping:** the cult-block is a **mid-radius terminus**; most players stop there, so it must read as "the answer" to everyone who does.

**§2.3 — The world: two forces, labels dropped (the dossier substrate).** The **source-fiction dossier** ("Order vs Establishment") is the fully-worked substrate; its metaphysics *is* §2.2 (a probabilistic "waveform" + a non-local "appendage/roots into probability space"). **The names never surface** (design-state §14); the player meets **meshed, linked groups**. **The institutional immune system** (dossier *Establishment*) — an unnamed apparatus across agencies/corporations/contractors that identifies, contains, exploits, and neutralizes the gifted, *seeing itself as protecting humanity from monsters while shaping the conditions that create them.* A **parallel, not a breakaway**; **compartmentalized** across every sector; its **shell ecosystem** (Pantheon Holdings, NeuroSync Labs, DDI, …) supplies the deniable contractors and the never-generic men-in-black layer. **The dispersed gifted networks** (dossier *Order*) — protection cells, old families, more connected, geographically isolated. **Duggan** (institutional founder, ~1890s; *distinct from Doug*) built a **snake-oil founding myth** (a captured remote-resource-locator finding minerals behind a fake contraption, sold first to Dutch royalty → the DDI "materials pickaxe"). **The cult** is a **rogue gifted lineage** — a self-hidden pariah the wider community disdains, in the cursed direction the men in black already **circle without understanding**; one of ~5 sector-contained dead-end mini-stories, the Explorer's the flagship.

**§2.4 — The sealed cast & mechanisms.** **Two leaders:** an **old** one — more powerful, of an **inbred bloodline** — **trance-bound in the cave adjacent to White's Hall** (he *is* the presence), who **scarred Doug (~30y)**, **took Ellen (raised as Tricia)**, and groomed a **successor** (less powerful → mobile/functional, **half serving the old man, half his own ambition**). **Tricia = Ellen** — taken because her **roots anchor toward leadership**; one fixture, two sightlines. **The knife (finalized):** lost years pre-game (**no seed, ever**); retrieved via foresight, placed mundane/deniable; **keys to *his* state**; a **deterrent that already failed** (found on a *return*); provoked, not looted; the run's **closer**. **The wake (decline curve, ruled):** Ellen taken + **Dale** spared at the same night (**~40y**) → **Doug** reached-for (**~30y**) → the player (today) — diminishing reach; taken-in vs reached-for-and-survived. **The shard, reframed** (Dean): the cave etchings are the **research center's private cipher**, carved in the cave; the player **records** it (a page — "the copy you made"), not chips stone — so on the return the **copy no longer matches** the wall (one mark more, the ellipse closes, no way to know which changed: the wall, the page, the hand, or the memory — the anti-noun turned on the player's *own evidence*). **Life-support & caretakers**; the (unreadable) **warner**; the **research nodes split by lens** — Nora's center (the powers · spiritual · past) + a **surgeon's group** (the brain mechanism · physics · live), never connecting. **The mind-controlled choice** (offered vs made-for-you, stolen-agency budget ≤1). **Foresight vs agency** (each outcome-arc needs a default stance; within-run only). **Denise's certainty** [compass-adjacent]: her dismissal is the wall she built so she'd never hold the unbearable alternative — that whatever took Ellen was never a man and is still out there. The game never rules on whether Dale did it, or whether Denise knows.

**§2.5 — The meta-layer.** One entity through **different people's eyes** (vessels = different characters, one per run). **The entity is the player.** Seeded only — the **▓▓** (the seam it can't read itself), **in-world lore**, **faction-scars**, the **fate-dial** (the recency window lengthening with depth), and **cross-run artifacts** (a seed like `denied_knife` / `dale_suspected` surfacing faintly in a later vessel's journal, never confirmed). **No-meta-reveal** is the wall; the journal + cross-run seeds is its natural un-blatant surface.

**§2.6 — The null pole, and its two faces** [the anti-noun at its floor]. Per the sealed cosmology **Dale is right — it's people** — and his grounded-human read is the *closest to true* of any frame, yet it rides the **skeptic/null** lens (§5): no tilt, can't bubble, can never win; the man who aims truest dies not knowing he was right (the game refuses to reward the correct player with the knowledge that they were, because that would betray the instrument). **Denise deepens this:** she is the null pole given a **human face and a human reason to need it** — so the null pole now has two faces: **Dale = clarity** (the dismissed truth-teller), **Denise = self-protection** (the terrified dismisser whose "it was a man" is fear-shaped). Same skeptic flavor, opposite emotional engines. And the felt-but-unprovable **doubles** at Denise: *did Dale do it* (never said) and *is her certainty knowledge or fear* (never said — she never says "I know," her hands never move). Percept-never-cause, applied to a person's conviction.

## §4 — CONVENTIONS & CRAFT RULES
- **Horror grammar** [LOCKED] — presence where there should be absence, absence where there should be presence, a skeptic explaining each away. Never a monster.
- **Percept, never cause** — enrich what's *perceived* so different priors each claim it; narration never adjudicates what *made* it. Answers **correct-answer gravity**. Extends to a person's conviction (Denise).
- **Percept-discipline as a wiring check** (the conversion became an anti-noun check) — *gate every callback and vindication on the flag that means the player actually **perceived** the thing* (the mark-coda on `cave_etchings_seen ∨ meeting_mark_seen`; the grave vindication on `grave_confirmed_empty`). An unearned callback is also a **false salience datum** that poisons the reads.
- **Conviction-voice, not narrator-fact** (§1) — cosmology-adjacent; the sweep is done (terminal reframed; stage-3's "You are being watched" survives).
- **The null pole** — `skeptic` = the **zero vector** (§5), not a fourth vertex; dismissal *removes* tilt (buys the unbiased world) and can't bubble. Hold the vocabulary at **four**.
- **The frugality tell** — the anomaly is frugal; **extravagant = off-model = a tell the author is someone else**. Applied to content: the `took_shard` variant corrupts the breather deck by *exactly one card*.
- **Seed discipline** — warm what pays off by recognition, cold what pays off by eruption (the knife). **Presented-but-unchosen options still seed** (every label is exposition — *why* labels stay neutral-valence). **Neutral labels protect the centroid** (a persuasive label tilts the player rather than expressing them). **Two channels:** what you *do* + how you *feel* both steer disposition; neither manipulates.
- **The anti-ham-fist / mundane-as-camouflage** (Loom) — the knife works because it was never mentioned; significant things hide inside ordinary ones (a real message in a column of "you still got that five bucks"), so the player almost scrolls past them, which is why they land. The breather-set logic applied to every surface.
- **Grip-banded both ways**; **grip renders as its band, never its number** (felt meters = band-words; clean feedback stays numeric). **"A failed roll is content, never a wall."**
- **The dignity guardrail** (the echo-character discipline applied to a *character*) — a figure whose blindness the game shows must have it **authored as lovingly as their brilliance/conviction** (Denise's fear as tenderly as Dale's clarity), or she becomes the game's tool to smear its kindest man. **The threshold cut-off** — the antagonist-turn ends at the *threshold* (the player becoming the thing at the end of Dale's road, conviction-voice, the act never shown) — for craft, the anti-noun, and responsibility.
- **The anti-noun of agency** — you cannot rescue the marked (Doug); and the **never-returned ending** (the anti-noun's quietest form; never a punishment for playing safe). **Defer-terminal** — no authored climax dies to the calendar guillotine (the calendar-end holds while a climax is scheduled in-flight; a guillotined climax also corrupts the telemetry).
- **The absence is the whole horror** (restraint as a content rule — the empty vault). **The density / casual-monstrous** (the horror is how casual the room is — the cake — offered to notice, never narrated at; it must *really be cake*).
- **Named beat-shapes** — the anatomies (A/B/C/D) · companion-reassurance-can-fail · the pressure beat · the density flip · the quarry pattern · artifacts-of-the-deep · dual-use faction content (the Z-axis, stance-not-truth) · real-echoes-not-real-names (the echo-character *demonstrates, never preaches*).
- **The authoring frame** — the tightening **spiral** (curves are the texture); dead-ends first-class; "find the spiral in the fog." **One fixture, many faces / reciprocity**; **shape-translation**; professions portable across corners. **Anti-noun discipline** — one core sample; route accumulation back to content.
- **Presentation-layer guards** (the invariants reaching the visual surfaces Dean's designing — each the visible-meter trap in a new costume; **the visuals are readouts of felt/derived state, never the state itself**): **the circle** = felt presence (who's in your life — a photo on the fridge, a coat by the door), never a scored/ranked panel · **the home** = qualitative, derived-on-read (the string-wall, the dishes reflect derived state, never render the position they're derived from) · **character cards** = faces, not dossiers (a portrait per name, never a stats panel, meter, or "true nature" line; never render the compass). *Instrument rider:* consultable surfaces enter the **presentation record**, so a scored panel would corrupt every future read, not just breach no-catalog.
